About Us

The Management Information Systems Association (MISA) is a student organization associated with the Beedie School of Business at Simon Fraser University, British Columbia. Our organization consists of dedicated executives and active members who recognize the importance of providing students with opportunities to learn more about the business technology industry and to participate in networking and social activities with local industry professionals. With over 800 members, MISA is continuously growing and coming up with new, innovative ideas for events. By working together with industry firms like yours to provide students with high-caliber events, MISA also provides you with an exclusive look at the future leaders of tomorrow’s technology management industry – your future employees. To accomplish this, MISA has paved the way with our signature networking event of the year – MISA Mixer. We have been recognized by the SFU Business Administration student body as the two-time “Rising Star” award winner for the positive impact our events have made on students. We intend to continue providing our MIS students with opportunities, while developing closer ties between SFU and the local IT industry. Our commitment and dedication is reflected by our continued success and we look forward to sharing that success with you.

What we do
